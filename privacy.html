Privacy Policy for Offra

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Overview

Offra is an offline voice translation application. This Privacy Policy explains how Offra handles information when you use the app.

Data Collection

Offra does not collect, sell, or share personal data with third parties.

Microphone Usage

Offra uses the device microphone to capture speech for translation. Microphone access is requested only so the app can process spoken input.

Speech Recognition and Translation

Offra is designed for offline translation workflows. Speech and translation processing may be performed locally on the device when supported by the app and operating system.

Offra does not intentionally store or transmit your voice recordings to Offra-controlled servers.

Analytics and Tracking

Offra does not use third-party advertising trackers.

Children's Privacy

Offra is not intended to collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and remove it if necessary.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@ogray.com.
